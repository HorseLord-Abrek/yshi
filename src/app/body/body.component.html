"Ready your napkins, tears will be shed."

After loss of her husband, Aygün is trying to discover the meaning of happiness to her and she looks for a purpose to keep on going by looking through her husband's memories. It is a drama animation about discovering yourself and letting go of past.
Primary Target Audience: Middle class females in university (18-24) from Turkey.
Secondary Target Audience: Middle class males in university (18-24) from Turkey.


Storyboard

Aygün is depressed and laying in bed. She is trying to find the will to go out.

She tries to go through her husband's memory book but she cannot proceed.

Mert visits Aygün at home and drags her out so that she can finish the book.


Aygün discovers crucial and unknown details about her husband Cantuğ while trying to repair the book.

Aygün finally makes peace with the content of the book and she decides not to repair it and leave it as it is.

Aygün finds the will she sought to make peace with herself and with the baby she was carrying.


Story: Abrek Atakan Koç

Art: Murat Ülker
